This document includes a comparison of Ethereum, Hyperledger Fabric, and Quorum platforms based on type, consensus, speed, token/smart contract support, and typical use cases. It also provides recommendations for DApp, supply chain, and banking use cases.

- Use Ethereum for public decentralized applications
- Use Hyperledger Fabric for secure, high-speed private enterprise solutions
- Use Quorum for privacy-focused interbank or consortium networks
